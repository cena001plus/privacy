# 账号删除申请 / Account Deletion Request

为了保护您的隐私，我们为您提供删除账号及其关联数据的权利。
To protect your privacy, we provide you with the right to delete your account and associated data.

---

### 1. 申请方式 / How to Request
请通过您的注册邮箱发送申请邮件至我们的支持团队：
Please send a deletion request email from your registered email address to our support team:

* **支持邮箱 / Support Email:** [cena008plus@gmail.com]
* **邮件主题 / Subject:** 申请删除账号 / Account Deletion Request - [您的用户名/Your Username]

### 2. 需提供的信息 / Information Required
为了验证身份，请在邮件中包含：
To verify your identity, please include the following in your email:
* 注册使用的手机号或邮箱 / Registered phone number or email.
* 您的用户 ID（可在应用内设置页面查看）/ Your User ID (visible in the App settings).

### 3. 处理时限 / Processing Time
我们将在 **7 个工作日内** 完成身份验证及数据清理，并向您回复处理结果。
We will complete the verification and data clearing within **7 business days** and reply to you with the result.

### 4. 数据处理说明 / Data Handling
一旦账号被删除：
Once the account is deleted:
* **个人资料：** 身份信息将从服务器永久删除。
    **Personal Profile:** Your identity information will be permanently deleted from our servers.
* **财务记录：** 订单记录将按法律要求进行脱敏保留，不再关联个人身份。
    **Financial Records:** Order records will be anonymized for legal requirements and no longer linked to your identity.

### 5. 重要提醒 / Important Notice
**关于订阅：** 删除账号**不会**自动取消 Google Play 的订阅扣费。请手动前往 Google Play 商店取消订阅。
**About Subscriptions:** Deleting your account will **NOT** automatically cancel your Google Play subscription. Please manually cancel it in the Google Play Store.
